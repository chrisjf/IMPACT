Collecting, processing and using personal data

You can visit our IMPACT toolbox without entering any personal information. We only store access data that cannot be assigned to a person, i. e. information on your internet service provider, the referring page or the name of the requested file. This data is only analyzed with a view to improving our services and does not allow any conclusions to be drawn with regard to your person.

Forwarding personal data

Your data will not be forwarded to any third party unless you have explicitly consented to such transfer of you data.

Personal Data

All information relating to your person is regarded as personal data, i.e. your e-mail-address, your telephone number or your IP-address. Information that cannot be related to your person does not fall under this definition.

For the sole purpose of safeguarding the secure service of this website we store your IP address when you open the website in your browser. This personal data will not be connected to any of your activities when using this website. In the case of any other personal data (i.e. your e-mail-address) we will always ask you for permission prior to collecting it. Personal data can be collected if you register on this website, subscribe for our newsletter or contact us via e-mail.

Cookies

This website uses cookies to provide a variety of functions. You can choose not to accept cookies from this website, but it might be possible, that some functions provided will not be working properly. The validation time of our cookies is limited to seven days. Prior to this period you can manually delete the cookies via your browser.

Anonymous or pseudonymous participation

All IMPACT tools can be used anonymously without need for registration. When you create content which displays information about the author, you will be referred to as "Guest". Alternatively you can choose to register on this website, for which just a username and a valid e-mail-address is required. Your e-mail address remains private and will not be revealed to any third party.

Argument Reconstruction and Argument Analysis and Visualization

You can use the Argument Reconstruction and Argument Analysis and Visualization tools to get an overview on argumentations regarding a specific policy. These tools serve primarily to store arguments from policy documents in the system.s database and to view the data in structured and visualised form. Using these tools requires no user account and no personal data is being collected.

Policy Modelling Tool

The Policy Modelling Tool gives you the opportunity to check the impact of various policies on your personal or any assumed situation. All data you enter is stored anonymously. Using this tool requires no user account and no personal data on you is being collected.

Structured Consultation Tool

The Structured Consultation Tool gives you the opportunity to discuss a policy proposal. If you choose to use the tool as registered user, your username will be displayed in your comments. If you choose to use the tool as unregistered user, you can provide an identifier for your comments; otherwise these will be published with the label "Guest".

Newsletter and Contact via E-Mail

You can subscribe to our newsletter on policy-argumentation.posterous.com to keep track of the consultation. The subscription requires a valid e-mail-address. To avoid misuse of our newsletter, we use the so-called "double opt in"-method which requires you to confirm the subscription via a link you will be send. Your e-mail-address is solely used for the newsletter and will not be revealed to any third party.
